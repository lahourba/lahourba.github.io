About Me

Diverse Experience in Operations and Business Growth
Over the past 4 years, I've immersed myself in the dynamic world of operations management, primarily focusing on young, rapidly growing startups. A notable highlight of my career was my tenure at Early Metrics, a pioneering startup rating agency. Here, I played a pivotal role in expanding our international presence, successfully spearheading the development of new offices in London, Berlin, and Madrid. This experience not only honed my skills in operations but also deepened my understanding of the global business landscape.

Passionate Data Analyst and Consultant
Since 2020, my professional journey has taken a deep dive into the realm of data analytics. I've engaged in various projects, each presenting unique challenges and learning opportunities. My work spans several areas:
- Designing and implementing comprehensive dashboards, with notable projects for clients like ENEDIS and SACEM.
- Leading data project management, with a significant project for Ouicar.
- Imparting knowledge as a Tableau & SQL trainer in academic and corporate settings, collaborating with esteemed institutions and companies such as DataBird, OpenClassrooms, HEC Paris, and emlyon.

As an independent data consultant, I've had the privilege of working with over twenty companies, including Enedis, Ouicar, SACEM, and DINUM. My expertise covers a broad spectrum, from aiding in the development of data roadmaps and establishing data architectures to performing in-depth analyses and creating impactful visualizations using Metabase and Tableau.

Educator and Mentor in Data Visualization
Parallel to my consulting work, I've embraced the role of a Tableau trainer. My experience has allowed me to share my insights and knowledge at prestigious institutions like HEC Paris, EM Lyon, and ISCOM, as well as in corporate environments. My goal is simple yet profound: to transform data into tangible, value-adding opportunities.

In my journey, I've always believed in the power of data to unlock potential and drive innovation. I am committed to not only harnessing this power for business growth but also to empowering others to do the same.

Projects

Building an ETL from Scratch

In-Depth Exploration of U.S. Retail Trends (2000-2020)
This project involved analyzing retail trends in the U.S. over two decades, focusing on data extraction, transformation, and visualization. Key elements included ETL processes, MySQL database integration, comprehensive sales trend analysis, and data visualization using matplotlib and seaborn.

Email Parser and Keywords Extractor

Python-Powered Email Content Analysis
A Python script designed to parse and extract key information from .msg email files. This included extracting sender, date, recipient, subject, content details, identifying top keywords, organizing information into a pandas dataframe, and converting it into a .csv file for further analysis.

Genius Scraping - Hennessy Mentions in US Rap Lyrics

Cultural Analysis through Lyrics Scraping
This project, 'Genius-scraping', focused on quantifying the mention of Hennessy in US rap songs. It involved scraping lyrics using the Genius API, storing data in a dataframe, exporting to CSV, and analyzing the frequency of specific words to establish a ranking of Hennessy mentions in the music industry.
